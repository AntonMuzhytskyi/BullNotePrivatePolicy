BullNote - Privacy Policy

Effective date: 16.04.2025

BullNote app respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, store it, and how your privacy is protected.

1. Data Collection

BullNote does not collect or transmit any personal data to external servers. All your notes and information remain strictly on your device.

We DO NOT collect:

  • Your name, email, or contact details

All notes are stored locally on your device. The app does not use cloud storage or any third-party servers.

3. Encryption and Protection

All notes are encrypted locally using a modern encryption algorithm (AES-256).

Notes marked as Protected are additionally secured using biometric authentication (such as fingerprint), if available on your device.

4. Third-Party Services

BullNote does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking tools.
